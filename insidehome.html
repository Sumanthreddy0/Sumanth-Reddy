Introduction

Microsoft's Home page serves as the primary landing site for the company and is designed to provide a seamless experience for users seeking information about Microsoft’s products, services, and initiatives.

Links to various Microsoft services and portals, such as:
1)Office (Microsoft 365)

5)Support
6)Business Solutions

Links to explore Microsoft’s wide range of software and hardware:
1)Windows 11 and its features.

4)Microsoft Surface lineup of laptops, tablets, and accessories.
